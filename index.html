WELCOME TO OUR KANTO 10 RESTAURANT! WHERE MAGIC BEGINS!

Welcome to Kanto 10, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality in the heart of Cavite. Nestled in the vibrant culinary scene of Mataas na Burol, Kanto 10 is a premier steakhouse dedicated to crafting unforgettable dining experiences.

  • At Kanto 10, we take pride in our commitment to quality, sourcing only the finest cuts of meat and freshest ingredients to create dishes that tantalize the taste buds and satisfy the soul. Our culinary artisans, driven by passion and expertise, meticulously prepare each dish to perfection, ensuring every bite is a celebration of flavor.
  • Inspired by the rich heritage of traditional steakhouse fare infused with modern culinary techniques, our menu showcases a diverse selection of mouthwatering steaks, from succulent ribeyes to tender filet mignons, expertly grilled to your desired perfection. Complemented by an array of delectable sides, sauces, and accompaniments, every meal at Kanto 10 is a symphony of taste and texture.

At Kanto 10, we believe that dining is not just about food—it's about creating lasting memories. We invite you to join us on a culinary journey that celebrates the art of steak and the joy of shared moments. Experience the epitome of steakhouse excellence at Kanto 10, where every meal is a masterpiece.
